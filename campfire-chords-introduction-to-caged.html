Campfire chords - introduction to CAGED

Brylie Christopher Oxley

Learning these five chord shapes will open up a deeper understanding of the guitar.

When starting out, we learn several common chords, namely C, A, G, E, and D. Those five chords (and a couple of minor counterparts) are used in countless songs. They also lay the foundation for a greater understanding of the layout of the guitar, forming a constellation that spans the length of the neck.
